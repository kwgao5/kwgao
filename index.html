I am a 3rd year chemical engineering graduate student at the University of California, Berkeley in the Balsara Lab. I am a member of the Joint Center for Energy Storage Research (JCESR) Solid Solvation Thrust and affiliated with Lawrence Berkeley National Lab. My research focuses on the ion transport and thermodynamic properties of polymer blend electrolytes for application in lithium batteries. More broadly, my interests encompass energy storage, alternative energy solutions, and optimization of processes. I am looking forward to pursuing startup and industry opportunities. Please contact me via email if you would like to collaborate or learn more about my work at Berkeley.
